LABYRINTH

1 Players
3 Cards

Move the tiles of the labyrinth around to collect the treasures. To win, one must collect all the treasures on the treasure cards and reach back to the starting position before the others. Controls: The extra tile can be moved around with WASD keys, and it can be pushed in with the Enter key. You can rotate it with E and Q keys. The current character can be moved around with WASD keys, pressing the Enter key finalises the position.
